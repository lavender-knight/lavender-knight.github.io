Why Art Direction?

     My interest in video games started when I was really young. Probably around the age of 5 when I was on the Wii playing Poke-park (A spin-off Pokemon game). I really got into it when the web-hosted games started getting really popular in the early 2000's as my Mom let me use her old Windows XP computer. I loved to play things like Webkinz, Poptropica, & almost any CoolMathGame... I'm not good at math so it's a good thing they dont really have any real math games on that site.
     Art has had a grasp on me since I was a kid, too, because my mom always supported me drawing and painting at almost any moment of the day. As I got older I realized there's so much art behind everything, including the video games that I've known and loved. It astounds me to see games developed where they expect your jaw to drop at the beauty and intricacy of their intentionally designed world/s. I know I've had those jaw dropping experiences many times over and I think that's a good thing to live for. Those things that excite you and make you want to know or do more. Art is what I live for!
